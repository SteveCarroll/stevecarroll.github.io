CJ has an interesting bio as an allegedly first black female self-made
millionaire. Born in 1867 after the proclamation, she was born free,
while her 5 older siblings were born as slave children. She did not
have an easy time getting to her success in black cosmetics and hair
care products and later her activism and philanthropy.
