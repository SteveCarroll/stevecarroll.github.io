Solving: instant discovery - 'don't even try to tell me different'


I had a harder time w/ the Jumble today; I had to employ the jumble
solver helper. Generally when I have one shorter word and one longer
word, I try to guess the shorter word and then figure out what to do w/
the letters for the longer word. In this case I couldn't guess the 5
letter word correctly, and I was left w/ a 9 letter word which was baffling.

The solver helper provides a *vast* array of all of the words which can
be made from the 14 letters, so I had my 'breakthru' from one of its
many 9 letter words which clued me to the correct 5 letter word.

Jumble solving always amazes me about how quickly some words pop out and
how slowly some others do. Example, this one was slow today BRAGEL.
