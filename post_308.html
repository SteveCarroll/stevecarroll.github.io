RC is 'chemically' mistaken or 'misspeaking' about bioluminescene vs
phosphorescence; they are not the same thing. Marine creatures
'fluoresce' (actually luminesce) by bioluminescence and the chemistry of
a luciferin and its associated enzyme, not the chemistry (and physics)
related to phosphor.

Since there are myriad different species exhibiting similar chemistry,
and many different luciferins, there is a bit of an evolutionary puzzle
there.
