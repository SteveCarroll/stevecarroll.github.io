'purpose' of the Republican power boss was to take TR out of the

it again w/ TR. 6 mo later WMcK was dead by an assassin's bullet that

abdominal gangrene and death 6 days after the shooting.

surgeon was out of town on another difficult case and couldn't/wouldn't
leave it.

inadequacies of the surgical theater and the surgeon, a gyn surgeon w/o
