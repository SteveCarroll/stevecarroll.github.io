Solving: right after ’that’s’ ‘small’ I recognized the quote.

NA’s medical care when he underwent quadruple bypass surgery at age 82 and subsequently died of medical complications that developed during hospital recovery was considered malpractice by his family’s attorneys. The hospital settled for a few mil. Scientific American mag did an article addressing the debate of whether or not such surgery should be done on 82 y/o.

NA was married to his first wife Janet for 38 y and they separated 4 y before they divorced. He married his second wife Carol at age 64 and until his death 18 y later. She was against the malpractice suit. She was 23 y his junior.

Besides his extensive naval and test pilot flying career, he did a lot of sail planing, including renewing his currency and gliding into his 70s.
