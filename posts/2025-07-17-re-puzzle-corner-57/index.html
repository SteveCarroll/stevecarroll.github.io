We don’t know where we’re going, but isn’t it fun to go? Lucy Maud Montgomery

Solving: I ‘zoomed’ thru’ the quote itself, and then the source had 8 of its own letters that hadn’t been used in the quote, but I was able to ’line up’ the unused letters and guess her name correctly.

LMM who wrote as L.M. Montgomery was the author of the popular novel Anne of Green Gables and MANY other novels, poems, and short stories. The quote is from The Blue Castle novel.

One of the things I liked about her bio, which I’ve seen in the bio/s of others is how her powerful imagination began working in her ‘aloneness’ at an early age. I wonder about why some brains do that ‘so’ – or maybe many of us also do it and we just don’t remember as well as she did.

Another thing interesting about her was that she saw herself as /successful/ from childhood; and consequently she was – her first book was an immediate success.
