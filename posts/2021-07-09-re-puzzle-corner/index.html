Mike Easter

July 9, 2021

Today’s CQ:

If you want to conquer fear, don’t sit home and think about it. Go out and get busy. Dale Carnegie

Another one of those motivational speakers and writers. How many billions have been made by the numerous such. Would you even check out a self-help book from the library? Much less spend money and time on it?

Solving was pretty easy, because I experimented w/ ‘if you want to’ and ‘don’t’ and that gave me a lot of ammunition, especially after Dale Carnegie popped out.

The Fri NYT x-word was hard and clever and enjoyable and I defeated it (not fast at all). My ‘system’ for solving Sudoku also worked well today; I never had to jot down 4 candidates for a cell. If there were more than 3, I made a little mark to indicate its condition. That strategy reduces bookkeeping and allows me to find ‘weak spots’ more easily because I can ‘see’ the ‘important’ whole grid’s numbers. If there were ‘4 or more’ numbers in there it would appear more complex than need be. Hidden triples are common; hidden quadruples, not so much.

– Mike Easter
