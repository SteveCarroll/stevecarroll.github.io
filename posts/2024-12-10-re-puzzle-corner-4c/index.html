Clue: Early electric lights Ans: arc lamps

I was unaware of the early tech of the carbon arc (was ‘arch’) lamp of (Sir) Humphry Davy. HD had a ‘varied’ background and the carbon arc lamp didn’t have a lot going for it, but some of the tech survives to this day. An interesting facet to the story is the bottlenecks which persisted for electric lighting because of the resistance and obstructions between the Thomson-Houston Electric Co & the Edison General Electric Co.

Humphry did a LOT of different stuff.
