All that we see or seek is but a dream within a dream. Edgar Allan Poe

Here’s the poem: https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poems/52829/a-dream-within-a-dream

I kinda like that one, as a non-appreciator of poetry, generally.

What I find ‘ironic’ or Poe-etic is the complete (IMO) mystery concerning the circumstances of the days before and the days of Poe’s death. That story gets a completely separate article from his bio in the wp. On top of that, some cardiologist wrote an article in a MD medical journal in ‘96 deriving the idea that Poe had died of /rabies/. That idea is more bizarre than some of the other ideas that were floated. There was no death certificate or ‘investigation’ and the doctor attending has been deemed unreliable.
