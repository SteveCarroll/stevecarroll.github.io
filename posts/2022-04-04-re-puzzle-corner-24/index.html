Re: Puzzle corner 24

Mike Easter

April 4, 2022

Today’s CQ:

RTV ELD PQ Y DVIRJVGYI; P TLFV RL DL RL TPQ TVYUVI; ILR GYI’Q. GYSO RCYPI

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

The dog is a gentleman; i hope to go to his heaven, not man’s. Mark Twain

Solving: ’the’ ’to go to’ ‘is a’ ‘I’ “Mark Twain”. Last was dog.

The online OCR had a lot of transpositions:

ELD PQ Y DVIRJVGYI; RTV P RL DL RL TLFV TPQ ILR GYI’Q. GYSO TVYUVI, RCYPI

I don’t know what’s up w/ that; I can’t imagine any mechanism that would do OCR and then jumble the result. It must have something in its algo that wants to make an English sentence based on word length or somesuch. IMO it should tend to its OCR and forget about the language. Let the user choose language or none.

There are some other good quotes about dogs or pets in heaven. Also, there is some controversy from those who believe in an afterlife about pets in heaven.

– Mike Easter
