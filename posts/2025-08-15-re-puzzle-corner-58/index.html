Reading about TR leads to McKinley’s assassination and the fact that the ‘purpose’ of the Republican power boss was to take TR out of the governorship by selecting him as the VP nominee. McKinley had handily beaten his opponent Wm Jennings Bryan in the previous election and did it again w/ TR. 6 mo later WMcK was dead by an assassin’s bullet that for some reason the docs were unable to find and remove leading to abdominal gangrene and death 6 days after the shooting.

One big problem was that the shooting was in Buffalo NY and the hospital he was taken was poorly staffed and equipped; the only experienced surgeon was out of town on another difficult case and couldn’t/wouldn’t leave it.

The wp article on the assassination has a very detailed recap of the inadequacies of the surgical theater and the surgeon, a gyn surgeon w/o the same skills as those of an experienced general surgeon. Bad job.
