Failure is simply the opportunity to begin again. This time more intelligently. Henry Ford

HF didn’t begin again his failed idea for a rubber plantation in Brazil at Fordlandia. Brazil gave him 2.5 mil acres and no taxes in exchange for 9% of the profits. Starting in 1926 the town was founded by 1928, but wasn’t abandoned until 1934. Well, I guess you could call the move to Belterra a form of beginning again and that was a viable community, but it wasn’t until the 40s that production became economically feasible, and by then synthetic rubber had arrived.
