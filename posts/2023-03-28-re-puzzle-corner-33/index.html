Back to Peanuts:

There’s a very long story, some of which involves Snoopy & Charlie Brown, behind the baseball story, which currently is an exchange between Patty and Marcie. You would need to know the characters to appreciate today’s exchange. Punctuation and letter case, C. Shulz’s.

Marcie (at the mound, where P Patty is manager/pitcher): Sir, how come everyone on Chuck’s team has a cap, but we don’t?

Patty: They’re a bunch of losers, Marcie! Which would you rather have, a winning team or a cap?!

Marcie (leaving the mound): Winning doesn’t mean that much to me, sir… I’d rather have a cap

Patty (alone on the mound, to the field): YOU’RE WEIRD, MARCIE!!
