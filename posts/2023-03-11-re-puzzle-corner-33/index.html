Mike Easter

March 11, 2023

Today’s CQ:

INAB AW ABRGAFNDYR. WHMMRJABL AW QIFAQBNY. – VNJHXA EHJNXNEA

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

Pain is inevitable. Suffering is optional. Haruki Murakami

Solving: I could only get as far as ‘pain is’ and ‘-ing is’ and an author whose names both ended in ‘i’. So I went to a crypto solver https://rumkin.com/tools/cipher/cryptogram-solver/#!/solve

It gives 1630 possibilities for the first word, 78 bad possibilities for the 2nd, 2 for the 3rd, 89 for the 4th, same 78 for the 5th, 106 for the 6th, and no help for the name because the gizmo doesn’t know about names, it is a dictionary attack.

The several word lists don’t ‘correspond’ w/ each other, so the remaining puzzle is to try to put something together. Obviously it was a huge help to get ‘inevitable’, but there was only the help of ‘context’ to be able to pick ‘suffering’ and ‘optional’ from their lists.

Then, w/ the quote, getting the author wasn’t hard. Others attributed have been Dalai Lama and M. Kathleen Casey. It sounds Buddhist to me, but I think HM is a westernized .jp.
