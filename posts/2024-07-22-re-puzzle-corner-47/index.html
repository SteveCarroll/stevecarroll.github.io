If you’re not barefoot, then you’re overdressed. Unknown

Solving: Quick. unknown, if you’re not, barefoot

I was trying to remember how long and how much I went barefoot ‘out’ such as to school and ‘generally’ in my schoolboy days. Quite a bit. Probably mostly in the warm weather from the 4th thru’ the 6th grades, which warm was most of the year. Warm summer weather also makes hot pavement; your feet have to get toughened up against pebbles and such also.

I made the transition from riding a bike to school to driving a car pretty early; never ‘did’ a school bus except for school trips. In agricultural TX in those years one could get a driver license at 14 if they took driver ed, which I did and soon thereafter I bought a car w/ the money I had made working, more in the summers than during the school terms before and after school.

Barefoot to bicycle to car took place over a relatively short time. Jr hi was 2 years 7th & 8th; I guess those two I wasn’t barefoot OR driving a car :-)
