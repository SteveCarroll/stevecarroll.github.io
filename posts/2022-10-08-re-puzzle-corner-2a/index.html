. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

Wisdom comes from experience. Experience is often a result of lack of wisdom. Terry Pratchett

Solving: a struggle. Finally I saw ‘often’ and ‘of’ which opened a window. ‘result’ ‘wisdom’‘comes from’ ’experience’. I wanted U>E which was right, but I also wanted other ideas which were wrong and slowed me down.

Never heard of any of that.

2007 age 59 he announced he had early onset Alzheimers; he lived 7 more years.

PCA is posteror-cortical atrophy, uncommon variant, the back of the brain shrinks

So, it seems to me that he let his ‘opportunity’ to be able to determine when his life would end ‘slip away’ from him and he lived ‘years and years’ in some kind of Alz condition before he finally died naturally.

That is a big problem for those of us who don’t want to do that. Both of my parents lived long past the time their brains weren’t any good any more. I do NOT want to do that.

I’m sure TP should’ve died sometime between 2011 and 2015, probably closer to the first half of those 4 years than the second.

The problem is that dying by your own hand ’nicely’ isn’t actually that easy, much less acceptable to such as your friends and family.
