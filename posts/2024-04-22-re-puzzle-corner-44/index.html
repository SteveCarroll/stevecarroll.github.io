You can cut all the flowers, but you cannot keep spring from coming. Pablo Neruda

Solving: all the, but, you can cut, cannot, coming, PN

PN is most often attributed for that aphorism and the article I like about him as the source refers to it as from Poetry of Resistance.

https://www.theparisreview.org/blog/2018/03/26/pablo-nerudas-poetry-of-resistance/

… but another site cited someone who worked pretty hard to find it in the pub/s they had of PN and couldn’t find it and favored a similar flower quote from Che Guevara. I’m going w/ PN; the saying has been emblazoned on banners of resistance demonstrators in Spanish and Arabic such as Arab uprisings and attributed to PN.
