Today’s CQ:

There are two lasting bequests we can give our children: one is roots, the other is wings. Hodding Carter

HC (‘07-‘72) was a newspaper editor and speech writer for JF Kennedy; he was moderate-progressive who somehow evolved from white supremacist to crusading for racial equality to ‘slow’ integration.

The wp credits HC w/ the above quote. Another quote reference had a lot more to say about the investigation of the source of the quote. Ronald Reagan mistakenly attributed the quote in one of his speeches.
