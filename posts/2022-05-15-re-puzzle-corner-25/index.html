If you’d really love to visit a certain country in West Africa, what is your choice? To go to Togo.

Solving: ‘if you’d really love to’

For organizational purposes, there are several similar ways to consider the regions of Africa, such as that of the African Union entity, North, West, East, Central & South. WA has 15 member states, of which Togo (officially the Togolese Republic) is a smaller one.

Here’s a nice little geography lesson pic for WA. https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/4d/Map_of_West_AFrica.gif/903px-Map_of_West_AFrica.gif

Togo is a very skinny country, has 8 million people, and the official language is .fr, but there are many languages there. From the colonial era of the 19th & 20th centuries there was (.de) Togoland, (read wp article ‘Scramble for Africa’), then British and French Togolands, then independence in 1960.
