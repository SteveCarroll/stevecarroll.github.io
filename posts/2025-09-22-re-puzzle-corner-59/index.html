A doctor Tucker “called in an otologist Dr Claisse, who on 10th October I900, performed an unspecified ear operation, probably a mastoidectomy for cholesteatoma.”

Since my search (including a medical journal on OW’s condition) didn’t satisfy me, I’m free to develop my own theory. I’m going w/ the cholesteatoma idea, because it can be congenital or acquired and is a destructive growth that could lead to meningitis and death particularly if compounded by unfortunate surgery.
