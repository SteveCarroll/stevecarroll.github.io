Count your age by friends, not years, your life by smiles, not tears. John Lennon

Solving: not, count your, years, John Lennon

That one is an excellent example of the work the Quote Investigator does. I went searching because I didn’t know/think JL was a ‘poet’. Those lines are from more lines of a poem written before JL was even born, and JL didn’t start getting attributed for it until after he had died.

If one hasn’t ever read anything on the QI pages, this is a good one to read, for the ‘intrigue’ or complexity of the chase.
