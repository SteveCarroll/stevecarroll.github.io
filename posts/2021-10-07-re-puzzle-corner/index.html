Mike Easter

October 7, 2021

Today’s CQ:

With the exception of world unrest, nothing breaks out in more places than an old garden hose. Weary gardener

There are several/ a number of/ entities that refer to themselves as ‘weary gardener’, so I wasn’t able to pin-point which of them might have been the intended source of the quote, and it didn’t seem important.

Solving: I wanted ‘??th the’, but I didn’t get far until I found ’nothing’ and ’than an’.

I saw an article today that contained the sentence “The report said an estimated 3,000 priests and an unknown (emphasis mine) number of ‘other people’ (my quotes) associated with the Catholic Church sexually abused children,…”

So presumably the ‘flaw’ in the stats to determine the number of victims per perpetrator would be based on how ever many unknowns the report is trying to ‘pin on’ the church to arrive at their vast number of victims. I’m thinking that somehow this ‘bunch’ in the commission wants to ‘force’ the church to make reparations to as many of the sexually abused in the .fr nation as they possibly can, whether the church was actually responsible or not. Deep pockets and all that.

I’m reminded of the boy scout business. I was active in scouting for a number of years and when I found myself drifting away from scouting before having achieved the Eagle Scout rank, I decided to ‘redouble’ my efforts to achieve that rank because I knew it would make the man I called my grandfather happy as he was a big time adult BSA awardee.

In any case, my point about the BSA is that it didn’t seem to me that the BSA organization per se actually did any ‘vetting’ of the various adult leaders I encountered thru’ the years; they were just men and women who were interested in scouting and volunteered their efforts. And no doubt some molesters crept into that role, but it didn’t seem to me that the organization itself would be so seriously damaged because it had ‘failed’ to perform some duty that it hadn’t even thought of in the beginning of its existence.

The BSA Boy Scouts of America is the overarching org of the cub scouts, boy scouts and explorers (that I was in my days). My father had been a boy scout and sorta ’trailed off’ at the Life Scout level (a level below Eagle), as I was doing and he never achieved Eagle. So that was another motivator for me to ‘get after it’ and get it done. That Silver Beaver dude was his uncle and who raised him and therefore was ‘sortofa’ grandfather to me.

And, I was glad I ‘went to the trouble’ of achieving that Eagle Scout status before I ‘outgrew’ the whole thing and got onto other endeavors; so it was actually more important to /me/ than anyone else.
