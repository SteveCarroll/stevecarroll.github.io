RMH’s website is a better source of info about him than the wp article. He also said, “I have a Disability yes thats true, but all that really means is I may have to take a slightly different path than you”.

RMH is wheelchair-bound because of limitations from a birth defect, spina bifida. He is a writer and poet, and also set a Ripley-Guinness record for the longest sustained wheelie of over 6 mi. Besides his website, when I turned to the Talk section of the wp article, I learned that he had initially written the article, inviting wp editing. He was also asked to carry the torch for the 2006 Asian Games.
