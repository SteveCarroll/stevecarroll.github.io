Sometimes I’ve believed as many as six impossible things before breakfast. Lewis Carroll

Solving: ‘I’ve’ ‘sometimes’ ‘as many as’; with those letters I was able to get the rest.

LC was the pen name of the mathematician Charles Lutwidge Dodgson, and to be more accurate, it was the Red Queen in Alice in Wonderland who made the quote as part of a larger one in a conversation w/ Alice.

What I find most interesting about the CLD story is the controversy raised by his numerous biographers about how his ‘sexuality’ worked in regards to conventional female interests vs some form of pedophilic attraction. I think the wp’s treatment of that area is pretty good, but one would have to read some biographies w/ various alternate points of view to get a better sense of it.

In the consideration of naked young girls, I recall a museum I visited which once had a roughly life-sized statue of a nude 10-11 y/o archer(ess) out front; but that has since been put away somewhere.

Here’s one picture by CLD of the naked Beatrice Hatch.
