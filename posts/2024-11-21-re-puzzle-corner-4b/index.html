My mother always used to say, “The older you get, the better you get. Unless you’re a banana.” Betty White

Solving: -’re a, banana, you’re, my mother

That quote isn’t precisely true; typically the grocery store’s bananas are typically ’too young’ to eat; they aren’t quite ripe enough. I buy some, leave them in their closed bag a few days so that they will ripen up. That is, one /could/ eat them in the store condition, but they wouldn’t be as good as they are riper.

However, then the BW quote comes into play; after the ‘peak’, the don’t get better as they get older.
