Geography lessons; I've realized several things I didn't know; the
proximity of the E coast of S. America w/ the W coast of Africa,
particularly if you include off-shore islands; that Panama isn't all
that flat, there is a mountain there, 12,000' from which one can see
both the Atlantic (Caribbean) and the Pacific and there are 3 similar
peaks in Costa Rica. I knew CR had mountains, having been there, but
not about Panama's.

Panama's climate and diseases have killed a lot of people over the
centuries, including Francis Drake. As far as I know, based on a 10 y/o
article, treasure hunters are still looking for FD's body off-shore
Panama. He died of tropical dysentery there (the bloody flux) and was
buried at sea in full armor and a lead-lined coffin and the 2011 article
said that the ships associated w/ the burial which were scuttled in
conflict had 'just' been found.

Since there doesn't seem to be anything since then; maybe the
body/coffin wasn't where the ships were.
