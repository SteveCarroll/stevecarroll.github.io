Interestingly SB only knows approximately how old she is, born in 1984,
apparently unrecorded.  Some of my relatives recorded birth dates and
such on a page in a Bible.  My mother was born 'at home' in a ranch
house, but she knew when it was and her birth was officially recorded,
ie she had a birth certificate. I call it a 'ranch' because there were
some animals there, chickens, cows, horses, pigs and no crops; so it
wasn't a 'farm house'. Also no nearby neighbors. She called it the
'home place'.

Oh, I forgot; and a *GREAT* dog. I lived there for a while as a child.
Two different dogs, a red chow and a German shepherd. My red chow had a
longer nose than the wp pix of chow chows. More like the b&w one of Red
Craze, except she wasn't as big as that.

My first dogs took care of me, not the other way around. Particularly
