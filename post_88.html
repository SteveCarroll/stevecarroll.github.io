Anne of Green Gables and MANY other novels, poems, and short stories.

One of the things I liked about her bio, which I've seen in the bio/s of
others is how her powerful imagination began working in her 'aloneness'
at an early age. I wonder about why some brains do that 'so' -- or
maybe many of us also do it and we just don't remember as well as she did.

Another thing interesting about her was that she saw herself as
/successful/ from childhood; and consequently she *was* -- her first
book was an immediate success.
