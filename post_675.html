I don't appreciate stupid poetry. 'They say' that poetry doesn't have
to 'make sense', but when it doesn't make sense, to me it is just
'stupid'. It is OK if an infant is babbling, but IMO song lyrics or
poetry have to have something to say that makes sense; it can't just be
some sounds strung together that 'appealed' to the poet. EA Poe could
put some good sounds together in a very sensible way.
