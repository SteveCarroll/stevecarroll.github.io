ML b. '59; while a 21 y/o undergrad at Yale, won a nat'l competition in
'81 for the design of the Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington, D.C.

The wp article on ML is of some interest related to her heritage,
controversies surrounding the memorial, later works and recognition.

In '94, a documentary was made about her which won the Academy Award for
Best Documentary Feature.

That turned out to be quite controversial as well, since it beat out the
popular docu Hoop Dreams, and there was much press about 'cronyism'
because of the role the director had in the Academy.
