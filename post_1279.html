As is so often the case, I found myself casting about to see if there
were some useful commentary on some context or other clue to what EON

it came out in '64.

In order to get the whole story, one has to read the article on EON, and
A Touch of the Poet, and More Stately Mansions, of which the article on
him is most interesting. His dying words, "I knew it. I knew it. Born
in a hotel room and died in a hotel room."
